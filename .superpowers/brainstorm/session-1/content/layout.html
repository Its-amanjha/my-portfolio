Redesigning the About Me Section

Since we are removing the photo/placeholder section, here are 3 visual layout options. Pick the direction that feels most premium.

A

1. Interactive Terminal Workspace (Command Center)

A full-width neobrutalist terminal widget. Visitors click tabs (e.g., profile.sh, capabilities.json, logs.log) to interactively read your bio, tech stack, and live execution status.

B

2. Neobrutalist Bento-Style Grid

A clean, asymmetric layout of multiple grid cards: a double-size card for the Bio, a card for Identity Telemetry (Education/Location), and an action card for the CV. No photos, just solid borders and flat shadows.

C

3. Typography-First Editorial Print

A minimalist print-ad layout focusing purely on large display typography, horizontal hairline rules, custom ASCII blueprint dividers, and structured text columns, completely removing card components.
